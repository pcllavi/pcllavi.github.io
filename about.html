it's nice to meet you.

my name is pallavi chandanala (puh-luh-vee chun-the-naa-luh). i'm nineteen and currently based in north carolina.

storytelling and human behavior have always been the focus of my passions, and art always my outlet. i believe that innovation is where these spaces overlap, whether that looks like human-centric design or a vivid imagination.

outside of the realm of product design, i love designing fictional worlds from scratch. much of that includes creating characters and coming up with their backstories, mannerisms, personality, and dynamic with others. sifting through different genres of music, fashion, interior design, cinematography, and architecture help me expand how i ideate.

learning and exploring in any aspect takes up most of my free time. i've always found myself drawn to evolution, dreams, strange pieces of history (i.e. the kowloon walled city), or how humans think (recently, nostalgia as a marketing strategy).

outside of that, i have been drawing for as long as i can remember!
